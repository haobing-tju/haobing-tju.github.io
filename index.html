About Me

个人简介

I am a master's student in Computer Technology at Tianjin University. My research focuses on graph mining, large language models, and agent systems, especially reasoning over dynamic graphs and cost-efficient LLM-based graph reasoning.

My current work explores how to enhance the reasoning ability, scalability, and efficiency of large language models on complex graph-structured and temporal data.

我目前是天津大学计算机技术专业硕士研究生，研究方向包括图数据挖掘、大语言模型与智能体系统，重点关注动态图推理以及高效的大模型图结构推理。

当前研究主要探索如何提升大语言模型在复杂图结构数据和时间演化数据上的推理能力、可扩展性与推理效率。
